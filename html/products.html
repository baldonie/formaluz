Inspired by the geometric shapes of constructivist sculpture, the Leah pendant light is a work of art that provides functional lighting. With a hinged adjustable metal modular frame, the Leah can be rearranged to create various shapes depending on your lighting needs. The faceted hand-blown glass shade houses a candelabra bulb, which produces a warm and inviting glow. This pendant can be dimmed to create a variety of moods.


Inspired by Italian Art Deco design, the Eleo pendant light features a bottom ceramic domed shade and an upholstered velvet top. This fixture is suspended from an oversized brass decorative chain that adds balance and elegance to the design. This pendant light is perfect for adding a touch of retro style to any part of your home or office. It would look great in a living room, bedroom, or dining room. Installed at different heights, a cluster of these fixtures would make a bold statement for any entryway.
